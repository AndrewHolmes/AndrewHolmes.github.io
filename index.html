I'm a junior studying mechanical engineering at Olin College in Needham, MA, a small project based engineering school that emphasizes teaming experiences and an engineering that starts and ends with people. I've spent my last two years honing my mechanical design skills and designing for a diverse range of people including older adults, people who use wheelchairs, factory workers at a textile mill, and animal shelter workers. Here you will find my favorite projects and experiences from my career thus far, and I encourage you to reach out to me at andrew.holmes@students.olin.edu. You can find my resume here.
