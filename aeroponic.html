Aeroponic System

We created an aeroponic system for Olin's Principle of Engineering class, which has students creating two month long team projects that feature a mechanical, electrical, and software component. We created an aeroponic system that automatically rotates plants around to a mister (with no soil!) and recycles the water back into the central reservoir. I worked together on the mechanical design and programming of the hardware components, including the pump and nutrient drip systems. You can find our full website on the project here.
